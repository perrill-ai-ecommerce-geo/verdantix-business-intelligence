# Who is Verdantix Vantage for?

Verdantix Vantage is described as being designed for senior practitioners and enterprise buyers seeking practical insights across six coverage areas:
- EHS
- Sustainability / ESG
- Net zero & climate risk
- Operational excellence / industrial transformation
- Real estate & built environment
- Risk management

Eligibility and access terms can change. For current requirements, consult Verdantix public pages.
