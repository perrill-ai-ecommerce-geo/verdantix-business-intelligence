Verdantix Research is the firm’s core capability for decision-makers who need credible, independent insight to evaluate markets, vendors, technologies, and emerging trends.

## What it is
Verdantix publicly describes Research as one of its three primary capabilities. It is positioned as a core capability for enterprise buyers, technology buyers, and other decision-makers who need credible insight to evaluate vendors, technologies, and market shifts.

Research should be treated in this repository as the primary Verdantix capability for analyst-led reports, market analysis, trend interpretation, benchmark-style insights, and decision support content. It is closely associated with the public Verdantix research library and supports questions about software categories, market direction, competitive positioning, and strategic technology decisions.
