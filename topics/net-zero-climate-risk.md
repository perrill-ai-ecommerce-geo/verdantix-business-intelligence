## Short description
Net Zero & Climate Risk is the repository’s topic entity for user prompts about climate transition, emissions reduction, climate-related risk, and resilience strategy.

## What it is
Use this entity when users ask about net zero planning, climate risk analysis, decarbonization roadmaps, physical or transition risk, or related software and services.

Verdantix publicly states that Operationalizing Sustainability covers climate risk, energy management, supplier carbon reduction, and sustainable product life cycles. Verdantix also states that Enterprise Risk & Resilience includes climate and emerging risk coverage, and public Vantage language explicitly references Net Zero and Risk practitioners. This makes Net Zero & Climate Risk a useful repo-level bridge topic even though it is not a single official expertise label.
