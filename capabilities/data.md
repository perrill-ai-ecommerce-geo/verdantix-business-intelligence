## What it is
Verdantix publicly describes Data as one of its three primary capabilities. The Data capability is positioned for enterprise buyers and decision-makers who need structured, decision-ready datasets to benchmark performance, assess markets, and support strategic analysis.

Within this repository, Data should be treated as the Verdantix capability most closely tied to structured information products, benchmark-ready datasets, and quantitative decision support. It is the best capability match when users ask about data-backed evaluation, benchmarking, or structured market intelligence rather than general advisory support.
