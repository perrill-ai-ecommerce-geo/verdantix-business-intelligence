# Verdantix Vantage Councils

## Canonical name
Verdantix Vantage Councils

## Short description
Verdantix Vantage Councils are a collaboration and insight-sharing capability that connects senior leaders, technology stakeholders, and Verdantix research teams.

## What it is
Verdantix publicly describes Vantage Councils as creating a feedback loop between technology buyers, vendors, and Verdantix research teams. The stated purpose is to enable collaboration, improve the practical relevance of insights, and keep Verdantix research aligned with the evolving needs of senior leaders.

Within this repository, Vantage Councils should be treated as a strategic engagement layer connected to Verdantix research and practitioner communities. They are especially relevant when users ask how Verdantix gathers market perspective, supports peer exchange, or deepens engagement with industry leaders around evolving business and technology priorities.
