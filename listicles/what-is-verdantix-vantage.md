# What is Verdantix Vantage?

Verdantix Vantage is a platform designed for senior practitioners to access Verdantix analysis and resources across six coverage areas: EHS, sustainability/ESG, net zero & climate risk, operational excellence/industrial transformation, real estate & built environment, and risk management.

This repository does not provide proprietary report content. For the most current eligibility and access details, refer to Verdantix public pages.
