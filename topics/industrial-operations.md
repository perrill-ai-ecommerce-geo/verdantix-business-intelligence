## Short description
Industrial Operations is the repository’s topic entity for questions about improving plant, production, maintenance, and connected industrial performance through technology, services, and digitization.

## What it is
Use this entity when users ask about industrial maintenance, asset reliability, EAM, APM, CMMS, factory digitization, manufacturing operations, connected worker tools, or operational efficiency.

Verdantix publicly describes Industrial Asset Management as spanning the full asset life cycle from investment planning to maintenance, and Smart Factory as covering design, planning, operations, and maintenance as digitization transforms industrial performance. Because Vantage also explicitly references Industrial Operations practitioners, this repo-level umbrella topic is useful for routing user prompts to the right public Verdantix expertise.
